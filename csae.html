ChatVeil — Child Safety (CSAE) Standards

Last updated: 2026-03-26

Zero tolerance: ChatVeil has a zero‑tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content and behavior.

What is prohibited

  • Any sexual content involving minors, including images, videos, audio, text, links, or solicitations.
  • Grooming, sexualization of minors, or attempts to obtain sexual content from minors.
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing CSAE material, or directing users to it.
  • Accounts that impersonate minors for sexual purposes or facilitate CSAE.

Enforcement

  • We remove violating content when identified.
  • We may immediately suspend or permanently ban accounts involved in CSAE.
  • We preserve relevant information when required for safety, legal compliance, and investigations.

Reporting

If you see suspected CSAE or exploitation behavior, report it immediately:

  • Email: logickosha@gmail.com (include screenshots, user IDs, and timestamps if possible)
  • In your country, you should also report to local law enforcement.

Cooperation with authorities

Where legally required or appropriate, we cooperate with law enforcement and relevant child protection organizations regarding CSAE reports and investigations.

Age requirements

ChatVeil is intended for adults only (18+). We take action on accounts suspected to be underage.

Contact
